Classic San Francisco Restaurant

We serve Pacific seafood, caught on small boats, and brought to us by local fishermen. We also offer a full bar with an extensive award-winning wine list to complement your meal.

Don't be crabby

Waterfront seafood restaurant & market offering fresh crab, fish, patio seating & San Francisco bay views.

The best places to get your buttery fix, whether it's a whole steamed crab, or another crab-focused preparation. Either way, arm yourself with the crackers and drawn butter, because we're going in.

We are dedicated to the finest local ingredients, where what’s fresh is what’s for dinner. From spaghetti with sea urchin on the Amalfi Coast to crabmeat roasted over a fire in a coconut husk on the Thai island of Koh Samui.
